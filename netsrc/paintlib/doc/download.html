The C++ class library: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintlib-2.6.1.zip (about 1,8 mb) or http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintlib-2.6.1.tar.gz (about 1200 kb).

Download this file. In addition to it, windows users need to download libcurl, libpng, zlib, libtiff, libungif and libjpeg for png, tiff, gif and jpeg support. See the links page for locations of these libraries.

paintX: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintx.zip (about 200 kb)

You can also download the suite of test images: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/testpic.zip or http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/testpic.tar.gz (about 1,4 mb either way).
